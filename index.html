Who We Are

At Elementia USA, we arm ourselves with the Core Principles of Driving to Zero, Sustainability, Efficiency, and Teamwork to drive Results. To do this, we engage our Key Behaviors of, Think Like an Owner, Being Responsible, Value Talent, Being Fully Engaged with a Positive Attitude. It’s these Core Principles and Key Behaviors that direct our efforts to deliver to our Stakeholders every day.

Our Mission

At Elementia USA, our Mission is to deliver for our Stakeholders (Customer, Vendors, Employees, Our Communities where we live and work, and our Investors). These are the “Big 5”. By creating interdependent relationships with each Stakeholder, we can build a sustainable business that provides value for all.

Our Vision

At Elementia USA, we are a combination of two great companies: Giant Cement Holding and Allura. Giant Cement Holding has been providing quality cement from 3 plants across the US East coast since 1883. Our Vision (Purpose) is “Building Sustainable Communities”. Allura provides quality Fiber Cement products in key markets across the US. Our Vision (Purpose) is “Making the Material Difference”. This purpose drives our ambition to make the difference every day, and that provides value long term.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is simply to Do Good Business. That drives who we are and who we do business with and how we perform. This creates sustainable value for our stakeholders and investors.
